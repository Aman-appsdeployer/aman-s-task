About The Event

Started in 2011, iPreneur, a summit for social entrepreneurs has become a crucial part of E-Cell. Students, academicians, industry professionals, and aspiring social entrepreneurs, from and around the globe meet to exchange ideas, collaborate, and explore the happenings in the world of social entrepreneurship.

It is conducted every year by E-Cell TISS, Mumbai. It provides a platform for budding social enterprises, industry experts, investors, academicians, and students from the space of social entrepreneurship to discuss ideas and innovations in a social start-up. Along with our Social Venture Challenge, we in the past have also organized several speaker sessions and workshops.
